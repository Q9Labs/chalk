# Google Meet-like Interface: UI Design Guide

---

## 1. **Main Layout Structure**
- **Video Grid**: Central area for participant video feeds.
- **Call Controls Bar**: Fixed at the bottom, centered.
- **Top Bar**: Meeting title, duration, and participant count.
- **Sidebar Panels**: Collapsible panels for chat, participant list, and settings.

---

## 2. **Key UI Components**

### **A. Call Controls Bar**
- **Buttons**: End call, mute, video on/off, screen share, chat, more options.
- **Placement**: Bottom center, always visible.
- **Styling**: Rounded icons, high contrast, clear labels (optional).
- **Visual Feedback**: Hover/press states, subtle animations.

### **B. Participant Grid**
- **Layout**: Responsive grid, adjusts for 1 to 16+ participants.
- **Active Speaker**: Highlight with a subtle border or glow.
- **Video Tiles**: Consistent aspect ratio, rounded corners, minimal padding.

### **C. Top Bar**
- **Meeting Info**: Title, duration, participant count.
- **Icons**: Minimal, monochrome, aligned to the right.

### **D. Sidebar Panels**
- **Chat Panel**: Right or left sidebar, collapsible.
- **Participant List**: Icons, names, status indicators.
- **Settings Panel**: Audio/video settings, layout options.

---

## 3. **Visual Design**

### **A. Color & Contrast**
- **Primary Colors**: Use a neutral palette with one accent color for actions (e.g., red for end call).
- **Background**: Dark or light theme, ensuring video feeds stand out.

### **B. Typography**
- **Fonts**: Clean, sans-serif (e.g., Roboto, SF Pro).
- **Sizes**: Hierarchical, with meeting title largest, controls smallest.

### **C. Effects & Animations**
- **Transitions**: Smooth fade-in/out for panels and controls.
- **Liquid Glass Style**: Optional translucent panels, subtle blur, and dynamic lighting for a modern look:refs[1-1,8].

### **D. Spacing & Alignment**
- **Padding**: Generous spacing around buttons and video tiles.
- **Alignment**: Centered controls, left-aligned participant list.

---

## 4. **Responsive & Adaptive UI**
- **Desktop**: Full-width video grid, sidebar panels.
- **Tablet**: Compact grid, collapsible sidebars.
- **Mobile**: Stacked video tiles, bottom controls bar, minimal sidebars.

---

## 5. **Accessibility**
- **High Contrast Mode**: Ensure all controls are visible.
- **Scalable UI**: Support for zoom and text resizing.
- **Keyboard Navigation**: Visible focus states for all interactive elements.
