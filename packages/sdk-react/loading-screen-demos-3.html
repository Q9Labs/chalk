Concept 07

Chalk Dust Vortex

An organic, physics-based simulation. Thousands of micro-particles (representing chalk dust or raw data) orbit a gravity well. Upon connection, they compress into a hyper-dense singularity before exploding outward as a shockwave.

Concept 08

Optical Aperture

A mechanical, cinematic nod to video and lenses. Overlapping physical "blades" breathe and rotate, tightly focusing the light. Upon connection, the aperture snaps wide open, letting a blinding flash consume the screen.

Concept 09

The Construct

Architectural and spatial. You are moving through a wireframe environment—literally "building" the virtual room. When the connection completes, the camera accelerates to warp speed, pulling you physically into the space.
