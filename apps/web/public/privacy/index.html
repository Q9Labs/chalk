Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Chalk provides low-latency video meetings. This policy explains what information we process when you create or join meetings, use chat, or interact with related collaboration features.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account and profile information you choose to provide, such as your display name and email address.
  • Meeting data generated while using Chalk, including audio, video, chat messages, transcripts, whiteboard content, and participant metadata.
  • Device and diagnostic information needed to operate, secure, and improve the service.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide and operate Chalk meetings and collaboration features.
  • Maintain reliability, safety, abuse prevention, and support.
  • Generate meeting outputs such as transcripts, summaries, and action items when those features are enabled.

3. Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers that help us run Chalk, when required by law, or to protect users, the platform, and our rights.

4. Security

We use administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information in transit and at rest. No method of transmission or storage is perfectly secure, but we work to reduce risk and respond quickly to issues.

5. Contact

Questions about this policy: privacy@chalk.com
